# Assault #

Theft is a deliberate act that causes a person to fear immediate physical harm or unwanted contact..

Real-time Examples of Theft:

  • Physical Assault: A student punches another student during a school argument.
  • Verbal Assault: A man shouts and threatens to hit someone during a road rage incident.
  • Sexual Assault: A stranger inappropriately touches someone in a crowded bus.
  • Domestic Assault: A husband slaps his wife during an argument at home.
  • Workplace Assault: An employee pushes a coworker after a disagreement.
  • Cyber Assault: Someone sends threatening messages to another person on social media.
  • Weapon Assault: A robber threatens a cashier with a knife while demanding money.
