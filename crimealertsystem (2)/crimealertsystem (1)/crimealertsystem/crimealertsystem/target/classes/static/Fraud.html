# Fraud #

Fraud is the act of deceiving someone for personal or financial gain.

Examples of Fraud:

  • Credit Card Fraud: Using someone else's card information to make purchases.
  • Identity Theft: Pretending to be someone else to access their bank account or services.
  • Online Scam: Creating fake websites or links to steal money or data.
  • Insurance Fraud: Faking an accident to claim insurance money.
  • Phishing Emails: Sending fake emails to trick people into sharing passwords or card numbers.
  • Cheque Fraud: Using altered or fake cheques to steal money.
